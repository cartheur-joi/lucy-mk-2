# Actuator Print Checklist

Use this checklist for each printable part revision.

Part ID:
Revision:
Material:

Print Date:

## Pre-Slice

- Confirm part ID and revision match STL filename.
- Confirm orientation favors strength along principal load path.
- Confirm supports are limited to removable and accessible regions.
- Confirm heat-set insert pockets face accessible side.
- Confirm minimum wall thickness and hole clearances still match spec.

## Slice Settings

- Layer height matches intended pass (draft, fit, final).
- Perimeters at or above structural minimum.
- Infill matches structural or cosmetic target.
- Brim/raft enabled only when needed.
- Temperatures and cooling profile logged.

## Post-Print QA

- No critical warping at datum surfaces.
- Holes and bearing seats measured against target.
- Fastener insertion test completed.
- Bearing/shaft coupon fit tested if relevant.
- Part labeled with ID and revision.

## Release Decision

- Pass for assembly
- Reprint with same revision
- Revise CAD and increment revision
